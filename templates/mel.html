Basic Cure

The cure for melanoma depends on the stage and severity of the cancer. It typically involves surgical removal of the tumor. In some cases, additional treatments like radiation therapy, immunotherapy, or targeted therapy may be recommended.

Diagnostic

Early detection of melanoma is crucial for successful treatment. Regular skin checks and self-examinations are essential. Remember the ABCDE rule when examining moles:

  • A - Asymmetry: One half of the mole does not match the other.
  • B - Border irregularity: The edges are uneven, notched, or blurred.
  • C - Color: The color is not uniform, with shades of brown, black, white, red, or blue.
  • D - Diameter: The size is larger than 6mm (about the size of a pencil eraser).
  • E - Evolving: The mole changes in size, shape, or color over time.

If you notice any suspicious changes in your skin, consult a dermatologist or healthcare professional immediately.
